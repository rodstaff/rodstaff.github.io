Here are some of the Websites/Apps that I have created.   Although they are interesting technical projects, I have also taken a few artistic liberties for your entertainment:   I hope that you enjoy the tour as much as I had great fun in its development!

Note:   Except for this website, all websites/apps that are listed below are deployed to a free Heroku account so the limited bandwidth will result in initial load times that may take longer than 10 seconds--sometimes even more.   Thank you for your patience.

ProSeed

"Pro-Seed" is a demo website which I coded from scratch in ReactJS in ES6/NodeJS/Express using Bootstrap and in-line styling.   It's a scroll type website.   If you like this and need help upgrading your website using ReactJS, let me know.

ProSeed2

"Pro-Seed2" is almost an exact replica of "Pro-Seed" above, differing only in the Team section photos.   That is, the total image/photo memory size (shown at the top or bottom of each image/photo) for ProSeed and ProSeed2 are 2973 Kbytes and 481 Kbytes respectively and this difference is the reason for the different load times.   Therefore, it is important to remember that ReactJS alone by itself will not make your website or app snappy and quick; so you will still have to optimize all photo/image resolution, among other things.

FamuTants

"Famu-Tants" is coded in ReactJS with React-Router and Redux.   Its playful nature hides its real capacity to make much more complex applications not only more manageable but snappier and quicker!

JustDoIt

"Just-Do-It" is coded in ReactJS in ES6/NodeJS/Express.   It is a todo list using CRUD functions.   A seemingly simple app that requires lengthy and tricky code!

MyWebsite

I created this website using a static site generator called Jekyll which is based on Ruby.   I chose Jekyll for its simplicity and for easy editing and uploading remotely on your local terminal.
